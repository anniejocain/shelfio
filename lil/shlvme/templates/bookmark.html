Shlv.me+

When you find something out on the web you want to add to Shlv.me, just click on the Shlv.me+ bookmark.

Supported Sites

When you use the Shlv.me+ bookmark, you can add just about anything on the Web to Shlv.me. If you send something from one of our supported sites, we'll try our best to extract details for you. Supported sites include:

  • Amazon
  • Goodreads
  • MusicBrainz
  • Open Library
  • IMDB
