FAQ

Whahuh? What is Shlv.me?

Shlv.me is an application that helps you build and share shelves of books, movies, albums, and most anything on the web.

I've installed the Shlv.me bookmarklet, what does this do for me?

The Shlv.me bookmarklet helps you grab content from Amazon, IMDB, GoodReads and MusicBrainz easily.

Oh man, I'm lost, you have a bookmarklet?

Yes and it's a good one. Find it on our About page

Your bookmarklet doesn't support my favorite site. Bummer. Will you consider supporting it?

If your site is amazing, we'll roll in support. Shoot us an email and give us the details.

To hell with you and your bookmarklet, I want to link directly to your "add item" form. Is that cool?

Yes. It's pretty straightforard. Use this as a template: http://localhost:8000/shlvme/add-item/?measurement_page_numeric=544&title=The+Information%3A+A+History%2C+A+Theory%2C+A+Flood&format=book&creator=James+Gleick&value=1400096235&link=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.amazon.com%2Fgp%2Fproduct%2F1400096235%2F&key=isbn&measurement_height_numeric=20&pub_date=2012

Do you sell my email address?

No way! We hate spam as much as you do.

How much does this cost?

It's free to you. Development is courtesy of the Harvard Library Innovation Lab.

How do I upload a picture of myself on my Shlv.me home page?

Profile pictures are managed by Gravatar.com. We like Gravatar. They'll host your picture for free and seem to be responsible folks (they're the WordPress people).

This FAQ doesn't answer my questions and I need to talk to someone. Do you have an email address?

Yup. Find us at Harvard Library Innovation Lab.
