FAQ

Whahuh? What is Shlv.me?

Shlv.me is an application that helps you build and share shelves of books, movies, albums, and most anything on the web.

I've installed the Shlv.me bookmarklet, what does this do for me?

The Shlv.me bookmarklet helps you grab content from Amazon, IMDB, GoodReads and MusicBrainz easily.

Oh man, I'm lost, you have a bookmarklet?

Yes and it's a good one. Find it on our About page

Your bookmarklet doesn't support my favorite site. Bummer. Will you consider supporting it?

If your site is amazing, we'll roll in support. Shoot us an email and give us the details.

To hell with you and your bookmarklet, I want to link to your "add item" form. Is that cool?
