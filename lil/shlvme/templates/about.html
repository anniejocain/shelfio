Shlv.me+

Once installed in your browser, the "Shlv.me" button lets you grab an item from Amazon, IMDB, GoodReads or MusicBrainz and add it to one of your shelves.

About

Shlv.me helps you build and share shelves of books, movies, albums, and most anything on the web.

Shlv.me is a project of the Harvard Library Innovation Lab.

Shelves are built using a jQuery plugin called StackView. Grab the code for StackView on GitHub.

Need more info? The next stop is to read the FAQ. If you still have questions, shoot the Harvard Library Innovation Lab an email.
