Shelf.io+

When you find something out on the web you want to add to Shelf.io, just click on the Shelf.io+ bookmark.

Supported Sites

When you use the Shelf.io+ bookmark, you can add just about anything on the Web to Shelf.io. If you send something from one of our supported sites, we'll try our best to extract details for you. Supported sites include:

  • Amazon
  • Goodreads
  • MusicBrainz
  • Open Library
  • IMDB
